InVision 系列基础教程：Sketch 设计工作流、成为一名产品设计师、UI设计基础、交互设计指南

InVision 推出过一系列 设计基础教程，均要通过邮箱订阅，每周会推送一篇。避免订阅等推送的麻烦，而且邮箱阅读体验不佳，这里整理里面的链接出来推荐给大家。

这一系列教程已经推出有一段时间了（印象中是2016年上半年推出的），每篇篇幅不长，但整体思路都不错。

Sketch 设计工作流


「Design Workflow with Sketch」by Clark Wimberly at InVision

  1. Introduction to Sketch
  2. Document Setup
  3. Design planning
  4. Type Systems in Sketch
  5. Symbols
  6. Creating a Hi-fi Prototype with Sketch and InVision
  7. Getting Approval
  8. The Handoff
  9. The Build Kickoff Meeting
  10. Post Launch

成为一名产品设计师

「Making a Product Designer」by Scott Hurff at Tinder

  1. What’s a product designer?
  2. The new design process
  3. How do you decide what to build?
  4. Why you should write your interface first
  5. Prototyping
  6. Designing contextual states
  7. Designing for thumbs
  8. The power of motion
  9. Shipping is an art–and a science

UI设计基础

「 Fundamental UI Design 」by Jane Portman at UI Breakfast

  1. What is UI design?
  2. Designing for the content
  3. Conversion and action elements
  4. The creative part of UI design
  5. User onboarding essentials

交互设计指南

「 Principles of UX Design 」by Timothy Embretson at Best Buy

  1. What is user experience?
  2. UX as a practice and the roles that make it up
  3. Project planning and process
  4. What is user research?
  5. What are personas and why are they important
  6. What is information architecture?
  7. UX visual design
  8. Presenting and participating in UX reviews
  9. Beyond UX

题图及本文配图截取自原教程网页，经过二次编辑配图。

最后，不得不安利一波 InVision ，不错的设计协同工具以及原型工具，深度适配 Sketch，而且免费。（只是有些时候国内访问会比较慢…）

其实，InVision 还是一家前卫且优秀的设计公司，不管是官网、Blog、LABS 实验室页面、以及推出的 Craft Sketch 插件、推出的设计师纪录片 Design Disruptors 等等，设计感都做得很不错。
